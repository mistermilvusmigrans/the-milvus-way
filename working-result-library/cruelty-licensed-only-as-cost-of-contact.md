**II. Cruelty, Contact, and Their Limits**

<h1>Cruelty Licensed Only as Cost of Contact</h1>

| --- | --- |
| - | [the-reciprocity-constraint.md](./the-reciprocity-constraint.md) |

# Statement
Clear-eyed cruelty, i.e. cruelty performed with full, undistorted contact with what it is doing to another mind, is not ruled out by this framework on epistemic grounds alone, since the framework's only resource for naming failure is incomplete correspondence, and there is no proof that cruelty always involves incomplete correspondence.

> **NOTE**: This does not mean the recoil an actor feels toward their own cruelty is set aside as irrelevant. The framework already holds that other minds have genuine interiority, and that failing to register it is itself an epistemic failure, not only a moral one (per [initialisation/the-baseline-obligation.md](../initialisation/the-baseline-obligation.md)). Recoil is therefore a candidate signal, owed the same procedure applied to any other felt state elsewhere in this system - ask what it is tracking, and whether that tracking is valid (per [initialisation/states-are-not-obstacles-they-are-data.md](../initialisation/states-are-not-obstacles-they-are-data.md)) - rather than obeyed by default or waived by default. In a given instance it may turn out to be the last channel through which the target's stake is reaching the actor at all, or it may turn out to be inert social conditioning with no signal underneath it. Which one is not decidable in the abstract; it is decided the same way any other state's validity is decided, on the specifics of the case. What the framework rules out is only *using the fact of recoil, unexamined, as if its presence or absence alone settled the matter* - in either direction.

What the framework does still constrain is *for what*. Since the one axiom gives no terminal value beyond correspondence itself, cruelty can only be licensed as the side-cost of an act that genuinely increases correspondence - the surgeon, the experimenter, the interrogator who needs the actual fact and accepts the cost of getting it. Cruelty pursued as a terminal satisfaction, with no further correspondence downstream of it, has nothing in this framework to anchor it; it is not forbidden so much as *inert* relative to the framework's single aim, and it predictably degrades the actor's own future capacity for contact (a habit of looking away from what an act costs another mind is itself a narrowing of focus, per ["I. The Base Pair", README.md](./README.md#i-the-base-pair)).

---

**Propsed generalised form, (for reuse beyond cruelty)**:

> Any act whose justification bottoms out in something *other than* the correspondence it produces is suspect, regardless of domain. This is the form worth applying fresh to new cases - laziness, secrecy, self-promotion, withheld information - rather than re-running the cruelty-specific reasoning each time.

# Derivation
Built directly on the axiom of choice (no terminal value beyond correspondence; see: ["The axiom of choice: the one axiom this system adds", foundation/system-foundation.md](../foundation/system-foundation.md#the-axiom-of-choice-the-one-axiom-this-system-adds")) and ["I. The Base Pair", README.md](./README.md#i-the-base-pair) (action as full expression, meaning the actor does not get to disown the cost by calling it incidental).

# Domain of validity
Tested specifically on interactions involving other sapient minds, where the further working-results under ["II. Cruelty, Contact, and Their Limits", README.md](./README.md#ii-cruelty-contact-and-their-limits) apply and substantially narrow the practical license this working-result grants in the abstract. This has not yet tested on self-directed harshness, which was explicitly flagged as needing its own derivation rather than importing this one unmodified.

# Open edges
Whether recoil, in any given instance, is tracking something real about the target's interiority or is inert conditioning with nothing underneath it has not been given a general answer, and the working-result does not attempt one - only the procedure for asking is fixed, not the outcome. This has also not yet been tested on self-directed harshness, which was explicitly flagged as needing its own derivation rather than importing this one unmodified.
