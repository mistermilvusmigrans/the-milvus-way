<h1>Context and Capacity Bound All Contact</h1>

# Statement
Full correspondence with reality was never meant globally, since no consciousness is in full contact with everything in every situation. Contact is always bounded by the particular context a person is in and the particular capacities they currently have. This is not a weakness in the framework; it is what keeps the framework honest about what a finite consciousness can do.

# Derivation
Follows from the bedrock fact that any entity, including a conscious one, has a particular identity and acts within a particular context; it cannot act outside what its identity and situation permit (see: ["The second clause: being and action", initialisation/the-foundational-conviction-stated.md](../initialisation/the-foundational-conviction-stated.md#the-second-clause-being-and-action)). Combined with the axiom of choice (namely that correspondence with reality is worth pursuing; see: ["The axiom of choice: the one axiom this system adds", foundation/system-foundation.md](../foundation/system-foundation.md#the-axiom-of-choice-the-one-axiom-this-system-adds")), this gives: pursue correspondence with reality as far as your actual context and capacity allow, not further, and do not treat the limit as failure by itself.

# Domain of validity
Applies to any consciousness, which, by virtue of being something in particular, is also finite.

# Self-application pass
**Checked against**: Whether reading psychopathy as "bounded capacity for one specific kind of contact" functions as a rescue for the framework rather than a finding. On inspection it survives the test used elsewhere (per [initialisation/states-are-not-obstacles-they-are-data.md](../initialisation/states-are-not-obstacles-they-are-data.md)): the boundedness claim is not asserted from what is convenient, but from what is independently observable in the documented case (intact factual modeling, specifically absent affective uptake, not general cognitive failure), the same shape of move used for sleep-deprivation elsewhere in this system.

**Not yet checked**: Whether this working-result exempts itself from its own claim, given that that I myself have bounded context and capacity too, and a harder counterexample may simply not have been considered yet. That is a live open edge, not a closed one.

# Open edges
