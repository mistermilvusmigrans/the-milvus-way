# Statement
**Core**:

An action is never a partial, distorted, or false expression of some truer self held in reserve behind it. It is the full and total expression of the actor as they actually are, in the actual context they are in, at that moment.

---

**Expansion**:

There is no hidden, more authentic version of a person sitting behind their panicked, coerced, or habitual acts, waiting to be revealed/exonerated. The acting self and the "real" self are the same self, under a particular context and/or set of constraints.

# Derivation
This is the law of causality, inherited whole from the bedrock metaphysics (see: (1) [foundation/system-foundation.md](../foundation/system-foundation.md), (2) [initialisation/no-exceptions-action-as-total-expression.md](../initialisation/no-exceptions-action-as-total-expression.md)), applied to a conscious entity: what a thing does cannot be separated from what it is, and its potential for action is actualised through its context.

The only thing this system adds on top of the inherited bedrock is the further observation that, since a conscious entity - insofar as it acts consciously - acts primarily through the faculty of awareness, and that faculty is fundamentally the faculty of focus, the *one* variable available to a conscious entity, i.e. the one place its own influence over itself can land, is where that faculty is pointed. ***This is focus***. It is not a separate capacity apart from conscious action but its prerequisite.

# Domain of validity
Tested explicitly against hard cases:

- Panic
- Coercion
- Trauma-response
- Habitual or automatic action

Holds across all of them without needing a carve-out, which is part of why it earned its place as a foundation working-result in this library. Not yet tested against edge cases like altered states of consciousness (sleep, anesthesia, severe dissociation) where the faculty of awareness itself may not be functioning in the ordinary sense; this has been flagged below.

# Self-application pass
**Checked against**: Whether the derivation smuggles in anything beyond the inherited law of causality plus the observation about focus. Nothing additional found. Both moves are named and cited, not asserted fresh.

**Checked reflexively**: This working-result implies that this document's own reasoning is likewise the total expression of my context and capacity at the time of writing, not a view from nowhere. That is accepted rather than resisted here. since a later revision under a different context is not a betrayal of an earlier "truer" position, just a different total expression under changed capacity. This is consistent with the versioning approach used throughout the directory of working-results.

# Open edges
- If the acting self just *is* the self in that context, the practical question of how to relate to someone (including yourself) after a bad act under extreme constraint becomes harder, not easier, i.e. there is no "that was not really them" available as a resource. What replaces it is addressed under the working-result [context-and-capacity-bound-all-contact.md](context-and-capacity-bound-all-contact.md), but the emotional weight of giving up that resource has not been fully worked through yet.
- Whether this working-result even applies to action taken while the faculty of awareness is substantially offline (sleepwalking, certain dissociative states); possibly it does not, since the working-result depends on consciousness directing itself, and there may be states where that direction is not happening at all.
