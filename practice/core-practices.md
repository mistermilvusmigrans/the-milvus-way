<h1>Core Practices</h1>

*Key load-bearing truths and practices to connect with them.*

---

# 1. The self as a system of systems
Breathe, move (e.g. exercise), sing a poem.

See the tie between body and mind, intent and act.

# No ontological split between soul/body, self/world... <br> *Yet, each is distinct, integrated but not merged*
Observe the world around you and how it touches your senses.

# Life-affirmation as a continual yet self-fulfilling goal
Acknowledge that every breath you take...

- alive in the moment

- looking ahead toward the potential for life

... is what every act is for.

This, right here, right now:

- Not separate from potential
- But tied to it
- ***A part of it and the culmination of it***
